Prototypes
Introduction oNext: Backlogs (‘Product’ and ‘Sprint’)
A prototype is a risk free demonstration of a product or system. Prototyping is a common technique in Agile projects as it is an efficient means of eliciting new and validating existing requirements via user feedback.
There are two types of prototype:
• 
The throw-away prototype:
◦ 
This is used to understand the requirements of the system and then discarded.
• 
The evolutionary prototype:
◦ 
This is further developed to produce the final product.
