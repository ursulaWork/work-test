Planning the Development

Planning the development of a product is an important activity that occurs frequently in the project. A plan may be needed for a long term view, for a release, or for a product map. This type of plan will be subject to change as the product develops. A sprint plan is a short term view and less likely to change.

What
After p in entry
The objective is to plan or estimate the number of sprints needed based on the content of the prioritised Backlog items.
Planning considerations could be based on:
• 
What could be considered a minimum viable product,
• 
What are the project objectives and success criteria,
• 
Business priorities,
• 
Capability of the core team (the first sprint could be a learning sprint),
• 
Complexity of backlog items.
The developer supports this activity by estimating the time needed for items in the backlog.
The test manager optionally support this activity by advises on test process.
The business analyst can support this activity by producing a Story Map.
