To support the realisation of the defined Artefact Set for an Analysis Project, a Product Map has been created. It describes the relationships between the artefacts to be developed as well as any dependencies between them; it also indicates a suggested order of development during projects, The specific set of artefacts to be developed will depend upon the chosen methodology and project requirements.
Artefacts are typically either the precursor of, or successor to other Artefacts, and may be dependent upon each other for their content and concepts. It is therefore important that the relationships between artefacts is fully understood by model authors.
