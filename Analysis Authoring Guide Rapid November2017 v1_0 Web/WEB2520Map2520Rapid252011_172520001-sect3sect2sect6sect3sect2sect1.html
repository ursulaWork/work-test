This section outlines the Core Notations for UML Stereotypes:
•  Stereotypes are a UML Extensibility Mechanism, appended to an Entity’s name within a Model or held within its metadata.
•  Stereotypes may be applied to Model Packages, Diagrams, Elements and Connectors, as well as to other entity types.
•  Stereotypes are typically represented in the format: ‘<<Stereotype>>’ (how the ‘greater than’ and ‘lesser than’ symbols are applied is dependent upon the CASE Toolset / documentation being used), and may also be represented on diagrams as appropriate.
