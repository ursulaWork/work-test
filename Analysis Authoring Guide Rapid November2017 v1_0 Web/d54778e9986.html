Planning and Estimating
IntroductionoSubsection/s: Business Analyst Cost Estimates oNext: Developing the Analysis Model
Planning and Estimating is an important project task. In the context of the Business Analyst role it is a useful tool in agreeing what is achievable along with the Analyst Scope and Definition Document.
Who
After p in entry
Business Analyst, Project Manager, Analyst Manager.
After p in entry
Why
After p in entry
The purpose of this activity is to provide information to the Analyst Manager and project on the effort and time needed from the Business Analyst on a project.
So that:
• 
The Project Manager can plan activities and when to engage with Stakeholders.   
• 
The Analyst Manager can plan and agree the Business Analyst commitment within Data Products.
• 
The Business Analyst can plan their workload. 
After p in entry
When
After p in entry
At the start of each project stage.
After p in entry
What
After p in entry
The Business Analyst should estimate the effort for their work for next the project stage.
The estimates are based on the scope of the delivery and business analysis artefacts to be produced. Stakeholders provide an understanding of the scope and from the scope it can be deduce which artefacts are needed. Additionally the particular development approach will impact on the artefacts.
During the early stage of the project estimates this activity needs to be carried out with the project manager as part of the analysis scoping work.
During development focussed project stages estimates can also be derived from previous products produced such as requirements and / or user stories by evaluating their complexity.
Estimates may need to be based on a fixed period of time for example a sprint. The estimate need to indicate what can be achieved rather than how long.
Any assumptions made to produce estimates should also be documented.
After p in entry
Outputs
After p in entry
Estimates of effort for the business analyst work and analysis scope and definition document.
After p in entry
 Hint: Use MS project to factor in elapse time to allow for holidays etc.
