Planning Stakeholder engagement is an import part of a project to ensure that the correct stakeholders are identified and consulted.
Who
Project Manager, Project Executive, Product Owner, Business Analyst   
What
Identify Stakeholders:
•  Who the key customers and suppliers are.
•  Which groups to involve.
Plan approach:
•  How to engage with Stakeholders, what techniques and tools to use:
–  Internal/external Stakeholders.
–  Number or Stakeholders to consult.
–  What questions to ask.
•  Who and when to engage:
–  Initial consultations.
–  On-going consultations during delivery (including product reviews).
•  Confirm responsibilities on managing key relationships (specifically the role of product owner and overlap  with Business Analyst).
Agree approach with Project Executive.
Why
Need to ensure that the correct Stakeholders are consulted to ensure the success of the project.
When
Early in project lifecycle & then as a continuous activity inline with the planned approach.
Outputs
In addition to analysis artefacts, the following can be produced:
•  Stakeholder engagement plan.
•  Questionnaires (standard list of questions to be asked).
•  Write up of notes from stakeholder meetings, workshops and interviews.
•  Meeting minutes: including actions and decisions made.
