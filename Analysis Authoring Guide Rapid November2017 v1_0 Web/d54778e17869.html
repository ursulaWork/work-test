Why (purpose)
After p in entry
The purpose of sprint planning is to agree the what is to be developed in a Sprint.
So that:
• 
The Core Team can agree on what can be delivered in the next Sprint.
• 
The Product Owner is agreeing what is the priority for the next Sprint.

What
After p in entry
The Stories are held in a Product Backlog until the Core Team agrees they are ready for development.
The Product Owner is responsible for supplying a prioritized product backlog for the planning meeting.
Each item is discuss with the development team, and the group collectively estimates the effort involved. The development team then make a sprint forecast outlining how much work the team can complete from the Product Backlog.
The Business Analyst produces a story map based on the work items to be developed in the sprint. They also update the stories in the analysis model setting the stories to accepted and produces a report based on the content of the story map.
