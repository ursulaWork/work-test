The artefacts described in the Rapid methodology allow for examination of an artefact to support the creation of another; e.g. a Stakeholder Business Model can lead to the identification of epic user stories. In an Agile development this can be considered a way of re-validating artefacts produced earlier.
Hint: The reader should consult the artefact product descriptions which indicate the inputs, peer and successor products.
Elaboration
Artefacts can be elaborated in two way:
1. An artefact can be produced with minimum information initially. The additional information is then added when needed (e.g.  adding acceptance criteria to a User Story),
2. An artefact is used as the basis for the creation of another artefact e.g.: breaking an Epic User Story down into smaller User Stories or Use Case modelling.
The important thing to ensure is that the artefact contains the information needed by the consumer.
